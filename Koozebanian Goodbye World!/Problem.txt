The Spooble is about to get drunked by the Fazoob. Since he knows that his death is imminent, he has decided to list all of his last words in a text file for the generations to come to see. Your task is to print out each of his last words, one per line.

Input Format

The input consists of a comma-separated list of last words, all on one line.

Constraints

There will be at most 200 words.

Output Format

Output each last word on one line. Do not include the commas.
